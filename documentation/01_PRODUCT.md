# 01. Product Overview: Aliado Laboral

> **"Accessible, Fast, and Transparent Labor Justice."**  
> *The first platform in Mexico merging Artificial Intelligence, LegalTech, and FinTech to democratize access to labor defense.*

---

## 1. The Core Problem
The Mexican labor system suffers from three critical fractures:

1. **Information Asymmetry (The Worker):** 90% of dismissed employees don't know when or how much they are owed. They sign resignations out of fear or ignorance, forfeiting their rights.
2. **Operational Inefficiency (The Lawyer):** Labor lawyers waste 70% of their time filtering free consultations from non-viable clients, driving up the cost of their services.
3. **Invisible Legal Risk (The PyME):** Small businesses often operate without proper contracts or regulations, exposing themselves to catastrophic lawsuits due to simple administrative omissions.

---

## 2. The Solution
Aliado Laboral is not a law firm. It is a **Three-Sided Technological Ecosystem** designed to optimize labor justice for all parties involved.

### 👷‍♂️ For the Worker (Protection)
- **AI Calculator:** Estimates real severance pay in seconds based strictly on the Federal Labor Law (Ley Federal del Trabajo).
- **Virtual Advisor ("Elías"):** A 24/7 AI chatbot trained in Mexican labor law to answer questions at no cost.
- **Defense Marketplace:** Direct connection with verified lawyers who accept payment on a contingency basis ("Cuota Litis").

### 👨‍⚖️ For the Lawyer (Efficiency)
- **Smart Filtering:** Lawyers receive only pre-qualified, economically viable cases (including estimated settlement amounts and preliminary evidence).
- **Complete Management:** A specialized CRM for digital case files and tracking.
- **Payment Guarantee:** Automated commission holding and payment processing mechanisms.

### 🏢 For the PyME (Protection)
- **Express Audit:** AI-driven diagnostic of labor risks.
- **Document Generator:** Ready-to-sign labor contracts, internal regulations, and administrative acts.
